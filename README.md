# NDIC-REPORT
Ndic Report using powerBi
# Desciption
This report consists 0f a dataset comprising of 15 rows covering various years (2010-2024) and 8 columns.
# Tools used 
Microsoft powerBi was used for this report, power pivot was used majorly for the analyses after initial cleaning of the dataset.
# Data analysis
we analysed this data by answering every required question appropriate for proper insight of this report; the first question required that we find the total number of bank accounts recorded across all the years , after proper analyses our finding reveal that the total number accounts as recorded from the data set was 2billion.

Accounts fully covered at #500, 000 over all the years combined were 2billion.

The year 2024 had the highest count of fully covered accounts.

Existence of a positive proportionate change, indicative of a  disciplined and good saving culture.

The growth in low balance accounts contributes substantially as its aggregated across a good number of customers.
